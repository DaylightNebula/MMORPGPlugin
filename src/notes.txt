############ Champions ############
Jhin/Hunter Inspired, Names: Rofu?, Thurne?, Thorne
Player uses a revolver with 4 rounds.  The forth shot before reloading deals extra damage.  Also has a sniper on his back.
1: Reload: Reload revolver
2: Smoke Grenade: Throw a grenade that explodes into a cloud of smoke.
3: Bear Trap: Throw out a trap, if an enemy steps on it, they are rooted.
4: Sniper: Switch to sniper with one shot, dealing large amounts of flat damage.  Very long cool down.
